## Data

The data used for analysis is included within the script. It includes the following fields:

* Year
* Total Revenues
* Total Shop Count End of Period
* Systemwide AUV
* Company Operated Same Shop Sales Growth

## Analysis

The analysis includes:

* Visualization of total revenues and shop count over the years.
* Time series forecasting for revenue.
* Clustering for shop performance.
* Additional visualizations for revenue growth trend, AUV trend, and operational efficiency metrics.
* Forecasting revenue growth for future year
